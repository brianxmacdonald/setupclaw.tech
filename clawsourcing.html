Questions

Things people ask
before they book.

What exactly is a "Clawsourced" AI employee?
A Clawsourced AI employee is a fully configured AI agent — built on the best available large language models, connected to your actual tools, trained on your business context, and deployed to run specific functions on your behalf. It's not a chatbot or a template. It's purpose-built for your workflow and designed to operate autonomously, the way a real employee would.
How long does it take to deploy an agent?
Most single-agent deployments take 7–14 days from strategy call to go-live. Multi-agent systems may take 2–3 weeks depending on the complexity of integrations. We'll give you a specific timeline during your free consult based on your setup.
Do I need any technical knowledge to manage this?
Not at all. We build and configure everything. Day-to-day, your agent runs itself. You interact with it through Slack, text, or whatever interface makes sense for your workflow. You don't touch code, servers, or AI models — ever.
What tools does my AI employee connect to?
We integrate with GoHighLevel, Slack, Gmail, Google Workspace, Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, ClickUp, Notion, Stripe, and dozens of other platforms via API and MCP connections. During the strategy call we'll map out exactly what's relevant for your business.
What happens if the agent makes a mistake?
AI agents aren't perfect — but we build in safeguards. Sensitive decisions (like sending a contract or refunding a customer) can be gated with human approval steps. The agent flags and escalates rather than acting unilaterally on high-stakes items. And our 14-day care period exists specifically to catch and fix edge cases.
Is this a subscription or a one-time fee?
Clawsourcing is a one-time deployment fee — you pay us to build and configure your AI employee. There's no ongoing subscription to SetupClaw. You do pay for the underlying AI model usage (typically $50–$200/month depending on volume), but that goes directly to the model provider. We don't mark it up.
Can I add more agents later?
Yes, and most clients do. We design the infrastructure to be modular from day one. Adding a second or third agent is faster than the first because your business context and integrations are already configured. Each additional agent is priced at $500 more than your base package.
What if it doesn't work for my business?
That's exactly why the consult is free. We'll tell you upfront if we don't think this is a fit. We've turned down clients when we genuinely believed the ROI wasn't there for them — because a client who gets real results is worth more to us long-term than a quick sale. If we take you on, it's because we're confident it works.
