Do I need to be technical?
Not even a little. We do the whole thing — server, software, integrations, and hardening. After setup, you just talk to your agent like you'd text someone. Many of our clients are non-technical founders who've never touched a terminal.
What's the difference between this and just using ChatGPT?
ChatGPT waits for you. Your SetupClaw agents don't. They run on dedicated infrastructure with a 30-minute heartbeat — proactively working your business, checking your pipeline, and queuing up actions 24/7 without you asking. That's a fundamentally different category of tool.
Is my data safe?
Your agents live on your own server — not ours. We implement OAuth middleware so agents never handle raw credentials, configure Docker sandboxing, set up firewall rules, and give you an instant kill switch on every integration. We set it up to pass a CISO review.
What if something goes wrong?
We start with minimal, read-only permissions and expand gradually. Chase monitors the entire fleet and auto-restarts crashed agents. You can revoke any integration in seconds. We don't hand you the keys to a racecar on day one — we ease in.
What about after the 14-day Proactive Care?
Integrations update, workflows drift, things break. The 14-day Proactive Care handles your launch window. Ask us about ongoing Managed Care on your discovery call.
